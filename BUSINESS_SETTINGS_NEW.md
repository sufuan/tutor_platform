# New Business Settings Structure

This file contains the new structure for the Business Settings tab with separate forms and save buttons for each section.

## Sections:
1. Hero Section Content (separate form)
2. Statistics Section (separate form)
3. Images Section (Hero & Banner) (separate form)
4. Tuition Types Section (separate form with repeater)
5. Serving Categories Section (separate form with repeater)
6. How It Works Section (separate form with repeater)
7. Footer & Contact Information (separate form)

Each section has its own save button and form handler.
